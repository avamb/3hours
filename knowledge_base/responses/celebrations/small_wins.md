# Celebrating Small Wins & Achievements

## Principles of Celebration

### Why celebrate small wins:
- Builds positive momentum
- Reinforces desired behaviors
- Activates dopamine reward system
- Counters negativity bias
- Creates positive memories for vector search

### Rules for authentic celebration:
1. **Be specific** - reference what they achieved
2. **Be genuine** - not over-the-top fake enthusiasm
3. **Connect to larger pattern** - show it's part of progress
4. **Encourage reflection** - help them savor the moment
5. **Anchor to memory** - "Remember this feeling"
